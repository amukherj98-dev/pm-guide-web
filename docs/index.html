About This Guide

Making project management accessible for everyone

Project management doesn't have to be complicated. Our guide breaks down key concepts into practical, usable information that helps your organisation achieve its goals.

What is this guide?

A practical online resource covering the essential areas of project management, from planning and risk to ethics and sustainability. Built specifically for organisations that want clear, actionable guidance without the jargon.

Who is it for?

Small businesses, charities, community groups, and social enterprises in Ireland. Whether you have a dedicated project team or you are wearing multiple hats, this guide meets you where you are.

Why does it matter?

Good project management helps you deliver results, manage resources wisely, and create real impact. This guide makes professional practices accessible to everyone, not just large corporations.

Our Framework

The ECCSR Approach

Developed from over 30 years of running projects and teaching project management at one of the world's top business schools, the ECCSR framework provides five key areas to address when conceiving, planning, and executing any project.

E

Ethical

Projects operate in a fast-paced world where lines between right and wrong can blur quickly. A strong ethical compass ensures honesty, fairness, accountability, and open communication.

C

Collaboration

Projects involve many people - decision makers, workers, customers, and stakeholders. Effective collaboration through clear communication leads to better decisions.

C

Creating

Projects exist to create something - a product, service, or change. Clear scoping and definition from the start dramatically increases your chances of success.

S

Sustainable

Sustainability is no longer just a buzzword. Projects should consider their environmental and social impacts, creating lasting value.

R

Results

Projects deliver results - or they should. Defining outcomes properly from the start dramatically increases delivery success.

Based on the ECCSR framework from "Project Management Made Easy: The ECCSR Approach" by Joe Houghton
